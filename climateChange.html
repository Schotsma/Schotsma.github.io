Climate Change - Interactive Website 2020

Role

Web developer, 3D Artist and UI Designer

Team

Me, myself and I : )

Tools Used

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Three.js, Cinema4D, Adobe Xd

Context

Climate change is a growing issue, I used information on the rising sea level and climate change from the 350.org website to create an interactive experience that could be used on the 350.org website.

Problem

How can an interactive experience make people care about climate change and the rising sea level?

Approach

  • Research
  • Story
  • Final

Research

I took information from the 350.org website and developed a story that would see the effects of sea level rise talked about on 350.org. Stories have been proven to help people remember information and are one of the best ways to connect to people’s emotions.

Story

I developed storyboards and a storyline for the user to follow through my interactive web page. At the beginning of the story the user meets a climate change scientist named Fitz who lives on an island in the north. He tells the user he has just discovered how to time travel and the user and fitz travel to the year 2200. When they get there, the world is drowning and Fitz’s home is under water. After traveling to other places that are under water, Fitz takes the viewer back to 2020 to show them how we could prevent that future. The user installs renewable resources on Fitz’s website and the two travel back to 2200 to see that the sea level hasn’t risen as much.

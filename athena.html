Problem

How can the UX/UI online educational learning platform be more beneficial for increased academic performance among elementary school students?

Approach

  • REB Approval
  • Interview Development
  • Research Sythesis
  • Solution Development
  • Wireframe and Prototype development
  • Final Prototype

REB Approval

Developed a proper REB approval paper outlining the purpose and benefits of our study as well as how it will be ethical. Any risks to human participation were outlined as well as our participation group. In order to maintain ethical standards, parents were interviewed to see how their children have struggled with online learning through their eyes. Information was kept confidential through the use of a locked cloud folder that only researchers could access. Participants were recruited through social media channels and were given a letter of intent outlining information about the study, risks and benefits prior to signing any consent forms.

Interview Development

Interviews were used as the main method of research. Open interview questions were developed surrounding main topics of online learning taking into consideration the coronavirus and how academic success has been impacted through transitions to online learning. Interviews were done virtually and recorded for later analysis.

Research Synthesis

Interviews were transcribed and then used to find themes. Coded themes were then put into an excel document which helped understand the biggest problems relating to online learning and academic success for elementary students.

Solution Development

As a team we used our research together to find the most common themes, create design requirements from them and then develop solutions for them.

Wireframe and Prototype Development

Then, after developing solution ideas, we as a team translated these solutions into wireframes and a physical prototype using Adobe Xd.

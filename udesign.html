Udesign Hackathon - App Concept 2021

Role

Team Researcher, developed main concepts for application and transitioned concepts into wireframes for our prototype. Developed the user scenario for app concept and worked with the team on visual design development.

Team

I worked with a talented team of designers, Daniel Moosa and Natalie Tan.

Tools Used

Worked with Adobe Xd, Adobe Photoshop and Miro.

Context

The Udesign hackathon is a problem solving competition open to all levels of designers. The competition awards first, second and third place to competitors based on the creativity of their idea, the User experience and the physical execution of the prototype.

Problem

How can we help students avoid burnout through connecting with other students?

Approach

  • Research
  • Implementation
  • Scenario
  • Final Design

Research

The first steps we took in the process was researching studies about how burn out has been reduced in the past. We found that social activities and interactions were a common re-occurring theme throughout various research studies. We then looked at students and research found that breaks between school work was also key to reducing burnout.

Implementation

We then started to translate our research into possible application solutions through rough wireframing. We decided that the problem was students often become so busy with their school work that they may not have time or commitment to take breaks. We decided we needed to develop an application that could plan breaks with other students without interfering with important school dates.

Scenario

We developed a user scenario to show how a student would use the app from syncing it with their online school calendar, to planning a social event.

Final Design

The app allows users to sync their school calendar with the app to display important school deadlines and upcoming social events called “Cooldowns” on the app. A user can chat with friends on the app and see their friend’s status. If a user is on their phone but not on the application, then their status will display as procrastinating. A user can also go to the invites page to view invites to social breaks and plan new ones. When a user wants to plan a new social event, they can select friends that they want to invite and then see what dates are recommended on the cooldown calendar.

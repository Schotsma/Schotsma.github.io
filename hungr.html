UI UX

Hungr - Future Interface 2020

Role

Director, Motion Graphics Editor and UI Designer

Team

Jenny Nguyen and Sara Nguyen acted in the prototype video.

Tools Used

Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects and Adobe Xd

Context

Developed a prototype video for a future interface that helps users track their eating habits and assists with creation of a grocery list according to the nutrients body needs.

Problem

How can we use technology to more nutritional grocery shopping decisions?

Approach

  • Research
  • Wireframes
  • Storyboarding
  • Filming
  • Editing
  • Final

Research

Researched what kind of nutritional information can be used to shape eating decisions. Gathered different nutritional categories and information which was later used in the future food interface.

Wireframes & Storyboards

Developed wireframes for the future interface with all nutritional information and images that would be on the final design. Then developed storyboards for the video. The story follows two people, one person with the device and one without, in order for viewers to see how Hungr could satisfy their needs when making a grocery list.

Filming & Editing

I filmed all scenes on location with signed permissions from business owners and recruited two actors. One actor would play the user with the future device and one would be without it.
